Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy outlines our practices regarding the handling of information when using the PulseLoop app.

Information Collection and Use

PulseLoop does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. All data entered into the app (including age, gender, and health risk factors) is processed locally on your device and is immediately cleared when you close the app.

Data Storage

We do not store any personal information, medical data, or usage statistics. All calculations and recommendations are performed locally on your device without any data retention.

Third-Party Services

PulseLoop does not use any third-party services, analytics tools, or data collection mechanisms.

Data Security

Since no data is collected or stored, there is no risk of data breach or unauthorized access to your information.

Children's Privacy

PulseLoop is intended for use by individuals aged 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect any information from anyone under 18 years of age.

Changes to This Privacy Policy
