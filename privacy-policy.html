Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of information the app collects, how it is used, and the measures taken to protect your information. By using the app, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

Information Collection and Use

Your health data is stored securely in Firebase. We do not sell or share your personal health information with third parties except as required by law or with your explicit consent.

Camera Permission

PulseLoop Health uses your device camera solely for the Heart Rate Detector feature, which measures heart rate using the camera flash and lens (photoplethysmography). Camera access is also used to take photos of lab result documents for upload. Camera data is processed on-device and is never transmitted to our servers or shared with third parties.

Cookies and Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand app usage patterns. This service uses cookies and similar technologies but does not collect personally identifiable information.

Data Security

Your saved documents, health data, and personal information are encrypted and stored securely in our backend database. Your data is only accessible to you and is not available to anyone else, including app administrators, except as required by law. Lab results storage is limited to 500MB per user account.

While we take reasonable steps to protect your information, no data transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure.
