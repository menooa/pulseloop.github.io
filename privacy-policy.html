Information Collection and Use

Your health data is stored securely in Firebase. We do not sell or share your personal health information with third parties except as required by law or with your explicit consent.

Camera Permission

PulseLoop Health uses your device camera solely for the Heart Rate Detector feature, which measures heart rate using the camera flash and lens (photoplethysmography). Camera access is also used to take photos of lab result documents for upload. Camera data is processed on-device and is never transmitted to our servers or shared with third parties.

Cookies and Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand app usage patterns. This service uses cookies and similar technologies but does not collect personally identifiable information. You can opt out of Google Analytics by using the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

Data Security

Your saved documents, health data, and personal information are encrypted and stored securely in our backend database. Your data is only accessible to you and is not available to anyone else, including app administrators, except as required by law. Lab results storage is limited to 500MB per user account.

While we take reasonable steps to protect your information, no data transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure.

Account & Data Deletion

You have the right to delete your account and all associated data at any time.

To delete your account: Open PulseLoop Health → Account Settings → Delete Account. This will permanently delete your account and all associated health data from our servers within 30 days.

To request deletion of specific data without deleting your account, contact us at support@pulseloop.tech.

Data deleted upon account deletion includes:

  • All health screening results
  • Body metrics and calculator history
  • Reminders (pill, appointment, screening, period)
  • Lab results and uploaded documents
  • Mood entries and gratitude journal entries
  • Step counter history and heart rate measurements
  • Notes and all other user-generated content
  • Firebase Authentication credentials
  • Firebase Storage files (lab results, uploads)

Data retained after deletion: None. All personal data is fully and permanently deleted upon account deletion. No data is retained after the deletion period.

Retention period: Data is deleted within 30 days of your deletion request. During this period your data remains inaccessible.
