What is PulseLoop IQ?

PulseLoop IQ is a social platform where people share puzzles, riddles, math problems, logic challenges, and trivia. Instead of scrolling through endless content, you exercise your brain while connecting with others who love to think.

Post your own challenges, answer puzzles from the community, follow your favorite puzzle creators, and track how your IQ skills grow over time.

Features

🧠 Daily Brain Teasers

Visual patterns, math, logic, word puzzles, and trivia from the community.

💬 Community

Comment, reply, and discuss solutions with other puzzle lovers.

🏆 Track Progress

See how many puzzles you've solved correctly and watch your skills grow.

📩 Direct Messages

Chat one-on-one with friends and other users in the community.
