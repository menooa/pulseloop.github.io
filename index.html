About PulseLoop

PulseLoop is a medical screening recommendation app that helps you stay on top of your preventive health screenings. Based on your age, gender, and risk factors, we provide personalized recommendations following the latest medical guidelines.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is my data stored?

No. PulseLoop does not store any personal information. All recommendations are generated locally and data is cleared when you close the app.

Are the recommendations reliable?

All recommendations are based on current medical guidelines from trusted health organizations. However, always consult with your healthcare provider for personalized medical advice.

Who should use PulseLoop?

PulseLoop is designed for adults 18 and older who want to stay current with recommended health screenings.

Usage Guidelines

1. Enter your basic information including age and gender

2. Answer questions about risk factors

3. Review your personalized screening recommendations

4. Consult with your healthcare provider about the recommendations
